常見問題

轉換失敗或一直轉圈怎麼辦？
請先確認網路連線後，於錯誤畫面點「重試」。整頁筆記的轉換通常需要 10–30 秒，內容較多時會久一些。若持續失敗，歡迎來信回報。
轉換結果有錯字或漏字？
AI 辨識手寫字可能出錯，重要內容請對照原稿核對。可於設定切換「忠實保留／自動修正筆誤」兩種模式；字跡清晰、光線充足的照片辨識率最好。
免費額度怎麼計算？
免費方案每月可轉換 5 頁，每月自動重置；只有「轉換成功」才會扣減額度。拍記 Pro 為每月 80 頁（公平使用上限）。
訂閱扣款了但 Pro 沒有解鎖？
請到「設定 → 恢復購買」重新同步一次。若仍未解鎖，來信並附上 App Store 購買收據，我們協助排查。
如何取消訂閱？
訂閱由 App Store 代收代付：請至 iOS「設定 → Apple 帳戶 → 訂閱」管理或取消，拍記本身無法代為取消。
Notion 同步失敗或找不到資料庫？
同步需要你的 Notion 中先有至少一個資料庫（database），並在授權時把它分享給拍記。可在設定中中斷後重新連接 Notion 再試。
我的筆記存在哪裡？換手機會不見嗎？
筆記與原稿照片只儲存在你的手機本機，我們沒有雲端副本。刪除 App 或換手機前，請先把重要筆記同步到 Notion 或自行備份。

FAQ

Conversion fails or keeps spinning?
Check your network connection and tap “Retry” on the error screen. A full page usually takes 10–30 seconds, longer for dense notes. If it keeps failing, email us.
Typos or missing words in results?
AI recognition of handwriting can make mistakes — always verify important content against the original. You can switch between “faithful transcription” and “auto-correct” in Settings; clear handwriting and good lighting give the best results.
How does the free quota work?
The free plan converts 5 pages per month, resetting monthly; quota is only deducted when a conversion succeeds. PaiJi Pro offers 80 pages per month (fair-use cap).
Charged but Pro is not unlocked?
Go to Settings → Restore Purchases to re-sync. If it is still locked, email us with your App Store receipt and we will help.
How do I cancel my subscription?
Subscriptions are billed by the App Store: manage or cancel in iOS Settings → Apple Account → Subscriptions. PaiJi cannot cancel it on your behalf.
Notion sync fails or no database found?
Sync requires at least one database in your Notion, shared with PaiJi during authorisation. Try disconnecting and reconnecting Notion in Settings.
Where are my notes stored? Will I lose them on a new phone?
Notes and original photos are stored only on your device — we keep no cloud copy. Before deleting the App or switching phones, sync important notes to Notion or back them up yourself.
